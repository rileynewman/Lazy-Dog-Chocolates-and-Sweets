The year was 2015. We'd been at our respective workplaces for over twenty years. We'd never owned a business and we certainly weren't expert candy makers, yet.
What we did have was a dream: to provide great candy and ice cream to a great community.
There were struggles and learning curves, of course, but with your continued support this past decade, we've been able to realize that dream and then some. Thank you for your patronage and making Lazy Dog a staple in downtown Grass Valley.

-Paula and Todd
